4th Annual

Why we clean up.

Cap Hill is one of Denver's most vibrant neighborhoods — and we want to keep it that way. Now in its 4th year, this cleanup is a tradition built by neighbors who give a damn.

Dadventure believes fatherhood is practiced through action — not just words. Bring your kids, show your kids, teach your kids what it means to care for the place you call home.

Gloves, bags, and vests are provided. Just show up.

👥

Who?

You. Your friends. Your kids. Your dogs. No experience needed. Just show up.

🗺️

How?

Meet at Whole Foods lot, 11th & Emerson, 9:00 AM. Everything is provided.
