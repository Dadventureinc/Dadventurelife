4th Annual Community Event

Cap Hill Cleanup
presented by Dadventure

Four years of showing up for our neighborhood. Join us the weekend before Father's Day.

📅 Sat & Sun, June 13–14, 2026
⏰ 9:00 AM – Noon
📍 Whole Foods, 11th & Emerson, Cap Hill
🎟️ Free to Attend
About the Event

Dads Who Give a Damn

Three years ago, a handful of Cap Hill dads grabbed trash bags and spent a Saturday morning cleaning up their blocks. No agenda. No funding. Just a belief that neighborhoods are worth showing up for.

Now in its fourth year, the Cap Hill Cleanup happens the weekend before Father's Day as a reminder that the best gift we can give our kids is a neighborhood they're proud to grow up in.

Gloves, bags, and good energy provided. Kids welcome. Dogs welcome. Dads mandatory.
